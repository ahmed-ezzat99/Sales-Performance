# Analysis Methodology

This document outlines the analytical approach used in this Power BI project.

## Methodology Steps

1. Data Import  
   The Excel dataset was imported into Power BI Desktop.

2. Data Cleaning & Transformation  
   Power Query was used to clean, format, and prepare the data for analysis.

3. DAX Calculations  
   Measures were created to calculate:
   - Total Sales
   - Total Profit
   - Profit Margin %
   - Sales and profit breakdowns by country and segment

4. Visualization & Dashboard Design  
   Interactive visuals such as KPI cards, bar charts, and trend charts were used to present insights clearly.

5. Insight Generation  
   The final step focused on interpreting results, identifying patterns, and highlighting business opportunities and challenges.
