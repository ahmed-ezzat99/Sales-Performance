# Business Questions

This project aims to answer key financial and performance-related questions to support business decision-making.

## Key Business Questions

1. What is the total sales and profit performance across all countries?
2. Which countries generate the highest sales, and which generate the highest profit?
3. Is there a gap between sales volume and profitability across regions?
4. Which business segments are the most profitable?
5. Which products contribute the most to sales and profit?
6. How do sales and profit trends change over time?
7. Are there high-sales products with low profit margins?
