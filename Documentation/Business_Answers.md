# Business Answers

This document summarizes the key insights derived from the Power BI dashboard analysis.

## Key Insights

1. The United States leads in total sales volume, confirming it as the largest market.
2. France achieved the highest profit despite lower sales, indicating stronger cost efficiency.
3. A noticeable gap exists between total sales (118.7M) and total profit (16.89M).
4. The Government segment is the most profitable among all segments.
5. Products such as Paseo and VTT are among the top contributors to profit.
6. Sales show a visible increase toward the end of the analyzed period.
7. Several high-sales products show relatively low profit margins, highlighting pricing or cost issues.
